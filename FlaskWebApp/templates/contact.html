HOW TO REACH HBTU EAST CAMPUS

HBTU Kanpur is situated in old Nawabganj area of the city. It is approximately at a distance of 9 kilometers from Kanpur Central Railway Station and Jhakarkati Bus Stand. You can either hire a cab from uber or ola or you can book an auto. Chandra Shekher Azad University of Agriculture & Technology, GSVM Medical College, Kanpur Zoo, Campany Bagh and Rawatpur Railway Station etc. are in close vicinity of the University.
